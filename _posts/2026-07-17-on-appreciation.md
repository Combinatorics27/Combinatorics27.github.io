most people (atleast in india) never receive appreciation. this i have infered from the sample of people on the receiving end of appreciation from me. 

the people who have valued the appreciation the most are the ones with relatively smaller friend circles. the ones for whom receiving appreciation is once a bluemoon. 

people with a lot of fame come to get habituated to receiving appreciation. the appreciation function is also dependent on the relative status of the person appreciating. you can be a Chief Minister having developed tolerance to appreciation from juniors but receiving it from the Prime Minister will make it to your memory.

another thing with appreciation is that its task/area dependent. you can be a popular star who has built a tolerance of fame but if someone (even a low status person) appreciates you for a skill (say gardening) that other people hadn't noticed, it'll feel great. it always feels great when people appreciate you for your niche skills and hobbies that other people fail to notice.
