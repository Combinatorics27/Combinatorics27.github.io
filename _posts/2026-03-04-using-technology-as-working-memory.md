i'm currently reading [The Art of Bitfulness](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/60154080-the-art-of-bitfulness) by Nandan Nilekani and Tanuj Bhojwani.

the first half of the book didn't add much value. but i'm glad i kept reading. 

one very good technique i've learnt is that of using any note app as a working memory for your brain. 

what happens now in the age of constant notifications, distractions and attention-stealing is that you see a lot of information throughout the day, mark many as read later or important but keep forgetting many of them becuase the human brain can only hold around 4 objects in the working memory (this i quote from the MOOC Learning how to Learn).
the same would happen with me. 

i used the technique today and it was helpful.
