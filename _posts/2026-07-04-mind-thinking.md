date: 2026-07-04

---

the usual way humans think - with their brains alone - doesn't seem to be effective. the brain has a small working memory. one can only hold around 4 things in the working memory at once. so for problems requiring extensive thinking, the brain alone doesn't seem to be enough. 

what is more effective seems to be thinking by writing. writing down seems to solve the working capacity problem and therefore makes possible sequential chain of thought reasoning in ways not possible just with the mind alone. 

i couldn't have predicted writing this sentence when i started writing this post. surely there are much better ways to think that i'm not aware of. but for now thinking by writing seems to be more effective than thinking through the mind alone.
