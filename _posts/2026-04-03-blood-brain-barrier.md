the brain is precious and so evolution has made a system where it is difficult for molecules in the bloodstream to enter the brain. many molecules that are produced in the brain naturally cannot be injected as in into blood because they cannot cross the blood brain barrier. 

so pharmacology has to work around this by finding other similar molecules or molecules with some other mechanism that achieves the same object.
