[false consensus effect](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/False_consensus_effect) is the tendency for each of us to think that our beliefs, ideology and even skills are shared by more other people than actually is.

we also tend to form social groups similar to our demographic and therefore that exagerrates our worldview even further.
